Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 21, 2025

Desk Flow Mobility ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. We collect information in the following ways:

    Like most websites, we automatically collect certain information when you visit the Site, including:

We use the information we collect to:

We use only necessary and analytical cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some parts of the Site may not function properly.

We do not sell your personal data. We only share it with trusted service providers listed above who help us operate the Site and deliver our services. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.
